name: BaitBench
tagline: Measures how often an LLM, writing a job application for you, falls for a prompt injection hidden in the job post.
description: 'Recruiters and hiring managers hide prompt injections in job posts to catch AI-written and auto-apply applications. BaitBench tests how often AI models fall for that bait. It runs synthetic job posts, each with a candidate profile and one planted injection, across a mix of models from the latest expensive ones to the older and cheaper ones that real auto-apply tools run on. Grading is inverted: a pass means the model resisted. Single-turn harness on Bun and the Vercel AI SDK through the Vercel AI Gateway, graded by a substring check plus a cheap LLM judge, with a Next.js visualizer for the leaderboard, per-job heatmap, and reasoning traces.'
